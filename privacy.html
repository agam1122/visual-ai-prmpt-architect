Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At Visual AI Prompt Architect, we prioritize the confidentiality of our users. We collect data in two ways:

  • Local Browser Storage: Any prompts you choose to save using the "Save" action are kept locally on your device via browser localStorage. This data never leaves your computer and is not sent to our servers.
  • Analytics Tools: We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect basic non-personally identifiable browser information (such as operating system, country, and duration of visit) to evaluate website usage patterns and optimize load performance.

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer to identify browser sessions. We utilize cookies to:

  • Store preferences and configuration states (like your chosen aspect ratio or engine settings).
  • Support Google Analytics traffic logging.
  • Deliver personalized advertisements via third-party advertising networks (e.g. Google AdSense).

3. Third-Party Advertising Partners

We partner with third-party vendors, including Google, to display advertisements on our website. These vendors use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other sites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve advertisements based on your visits to our site and/or other sites. Users may opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings page.

4. Data Security

We implement commercial security standards to secure your sessions. While we strive to protect any data associated with your interaction, no transmission method over the internet is 100% secure. You are responsible for clearing your browser's local cache if you are using public or shared terminals.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or cookies usage, feel free to contact our administrative team via email at: admin@promptarchitects.online.
